Unveiling Real-World Applications

🌟 Webinar Recap 🌟

Date & Time:

February 10th, 2024
9:30 am - 11:30 am

Guest Speaker:

Anirban Pal

Attendees:

115

Introduction:

The Cloud Club, in collaboration with the CSI Chapter, organized a highly informative webinar on February 10th, 2024. The webinar focused on exploring the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and related topics, providing valuable insights into cloud computing solutions. ☁️💻

Agenda Overview:

  1. Introduction to Cloud Computing
  2. Overview of Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
  3. Deep Dive into GCP Services and Features
  4. Virtual Machines (VMs) in GCP
  5. Q&A Session

Key Highlights:

Introduction to Cloud Computing:

Anirban Pal provided a comprehensive introduction to cloud computing, explaining its benefits, key concepts, and the shift towards cloud-based solutions in today's digital landscape. 🌐📊

Overview of Google Cloud Platform (GCP):

The webinar extensively covered the GCP ecosystem, including its core services. Participants gained insights into how GCP enables scalable, flexible, and cost-effective cloud solutions. 🌐💡

Virtual Machines (VMs) in GCP:

The webinar highlighted the importance of VMs in cloud computing and provided practical examples of VM deployment, management, and optimization within the GCP environment. 💻🔧

Q&A Session:

A lively question and answer session allowed participants to engage with Anirban Pal, seeking clarification on various GCP topics, best practices, and industry trends. 🙋‍♂️🙋‍♀️
